## CATEGORY 1: FOUNDATION & SETUP (Pre-Launch - Weeks 1-2)

### Market Research & Analysis
- **T001**: Research SSI population in your state (use ChatGPT/AI)
- **T002**: Research Social Security Retirement population in your state
- **T003**: Research VA disability recipients in your state (10%-100% ratings)
- **T004**: Count social workers in your area (50-mile radius)
- **T005**: Count case managers/discharge planners in your area
- **T006**: Count Department of Corrections release numbers monthly
- **T007**: Research fixed-income population at risk of homelessness in your city
- **T008**: Research Baby Boomer population turning 65 daily (through 2040)
- **T009**: Identify halfway houses in your area (and wait list length)
- **T010**: Map 50-mile radius target areas (rural areas without services)
- **T011**: Research personal needs allowance for your state
- **T012**: Pull VA disability rate and payee charts
- **T013**: Conduct SWOT analysis quarterly

### Business Structure & Legal
- **T014**: Choose business structure (LLC, S-Corp, etc.)
- **T015**: Register business name
- **T016**: Get EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- **T017**: Open business bank account
- **T018**: Set up merchant account for payments
- **T019**: Get business insurance (general liability, property)
- **T020**: Create digital footprint (website, Google Business Profile)
- **T021**: Set up business phone number (separate from personal)
- **T022**: Create business email
- **T023**: Understand unlicensed home requirements for your state
- **T024**: Verify zoning laws for target neighborhoods
- **T025**: Create operating policies and procedures (SOPs)

### Vision & Goal Setting
- **T026**: Complete Action Indicator Assessment (10 categories, 75/100 minimum)
- **T027**: Define property vision (type, beds, location, demographics)
- **T028**: Set 90-day acquisition goal
- **T029**: Determine acquisition strategy (rental arbitrage vs purchase)
- **T030**: Calculate startup capital needed ($7k arbitrage vs $40k+ purchase)
- **T031**: Create Model Week schedule (entire life mapping)
- **T032**: Block P.R.O.T.E.C.T. practice times (daily 10 min non-negotiable)
- **T033**: Identify personal distractions and elimination strategies

## CATEGORY 2: PROPERTY ACQUISITION (Weeks 3-6)

### Property Search
- **T034**: Set up Zillow alerts for target area
- **T035**: Set up Apartments.com alerts
- **T036**: Set up Craigslist alerts
- **T037**: Drive target neighborhoods for "For Rent" signs
- **T038**: Photo document properties during neighborhood drives
- **T039**: Identify 5 target neighborhoods
- **T040**: Contact 20+ landlords per week
- **T041**: Schedule property tours (minimum 3 per week)
- **T042**: Run underwriting on 5+ properties weekly

### Rental Arbitrage Strategy
- **T043**: Calculate monthly lease cost vs bed revenue potential
- **T044**: Budget for security deposit (typically 1-2 months rent)
- **T045**: Budget for setup costs ($2,500 furniture/supplies max)
- **T046**: Budget for operating reserve ($2,000 minimum)
- **T047**: Ensure property allows subletting (verify lease terms)
- **T048**: Negotiate landlord terms (early termination, maintenance)

### Landlord Outreach & Pitch
- **T049**: Create landlord pitch script
- **T050**: Prepare landlord pitch presentation deck
- **T051**: Highlight benefits: guaranteed rent, longer tenancy, property maintenance
- **T052**: Offer to handle all tenant screening
- **T053**: Provide proof of business insurance
- **T054**: Show business bank account statements
- **T055**: Provide personal and business references
- **T056**: Offer rent premium if needed (e.g., $100-200 above asking)
- **T057**: Sign lease agreement
- **T058**: Get landlord contact for emergencies

### Property Purchase Strategy (Alternative)
- **T059**: Research seller financing opportunities
- **T060**: Research subject-to deals
- **T061**: Research wrap-around mortgages
- **T062**: Research lease options
- **T063**: Connect with Dion Lewis for creative financing training
- **T064**: Calculate down payment needed (traditional)
- **T065**: Get pre-approved for mortgage
- **T066**: Avoid rental arbitrage long-term (build equity instead)

## CATEGORY 3: PROPERTY SETUP & FURNISHING (Weeks 5-7)

### Furniture & Supplies (Under $2,500 Total)
- **T067**: Source used furniture from Facebook Marketplace
- **T068**: Target upper-class neighborhoods for quality used items
- **T069**: Check Goodwill for low-cost furniture
- **T070**: Accept furniture donations (post on social media)
- **T071**: Buy beds (2 per room for shared rooms)
- **T072**: Buy dressers/nightstands (1 per resident)
- **T073**: Buy lamps for each bed
- **T074**: Buy sheets, pillows, blankets (skip decorative pillows - they become sleep pillows)
- **T075**: Stage beds with simple decor (Family Dollar $3 wall art)
- **T076**: Furnish common areas (couch, TV, dining table)
- **T077**: Stock kitchen basics (plates, utensils, pots, pans)
- **T078**: DON'T overspend on decor (residents change/damage items)
- **T079**: Match furnishing level to target demographic income level

### Safety & Compliance
- **T080**: Install smoke detectors in every room
- **T081**: Install carbon monoxide detectors
- **T082**: Install fire extinguishers (kitchen, hallways)
- **T083**: Create fire evacuation plan (post in common areas)
- **T084**: Ensure all residents can self-evacuate (legal requirement)
- **T085**: Install locks on bedroom doors (privacy + security)
- **T086**: Install security cameras (common areas only, not bedrooms)
- **T087**: Ensure wheelchair accessibility if serving mobility-limited residents
- **T088**: Check water heater temperature (prevent scalding)
- **T089**: Secure hazardous materials (cleaning supplies)
- **T090**: Test all outlets and electrical systems

### Utilities & Services Setup
- **T091**: Set up electricity in business name
- **T092**: Set up gas in business name
- **T093**: Set up water/sewer in business name
- **T094**: Set up internet ($100/month budget)
- **T095**: Set up cable/streaming services (optional)
- **T096**: Set up trash collection
- **T097**: Establish lawn care provider (or handle in-house)
- **T098**: Establish pest control provider (quarterly minimum)
- **T099**: Establish HVAC maintenance provider

## CATEGORY 7: FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT (Ongoing)

### Pricing Strategy
- **T258**: Research personal needs allowance for your state
- **T259**: Price beds to leave resident with PNA
- **T260**: Example: SSI $967 - PNA $60 = $907 maximum rent
- **T261**: Set competitive rates ($650-$850/month Houston benchmark)
- **T262**: Offer shared room vs private room pricing tiers
- **T263**: Calculate break-even occupancy (typically 70-75%)
- **T264**: Aim for max capacity cash flow
- **T265**: Example: 6 beds x $800 = $4,800 gross/month

### Budget & Expense Management
- **T266**: Track all income in accounting software
- **T267**: Track all expenses by category
- **T268**: Calculate monthly lease cost (rental arbitrage)
- **T269**: Budget utilities ($400-600/month for 6-bed home)
- **T270**: Budget internet ($100/month)
- **T271**: Budget maintenance ($150/month reserve)
- **T272**: Budget insurance ($200/month)
- **T273**: Budget supplies ($100/month)
- **T274**: Budget marketing ($50/month)
- **T275**: Budget miscellaneous ($100/month buffer)
- **T276**: Apply 15% vacancy buffer to projections
- **T277**: Separate business and personal finances 100%

### Revenue Optimization
- **T278**: Target residents with SS (higher income than SSI)
- **T279**: Target VA disability 70%+ ratings ($1,700-3,700/month)
- **T280**: Fill beds to max capacity ASAP
- **T281**: Maintain 95%+ occupancy rate
- **T282**: Minimize turnover (returning citizens stay longer)
- **T283**: Offer move-in specials (first month discount)
- **T284**: Never stop marketing (even at max capacity for referrals)

### Scaling & Growth
- **T285**: Use first property profits to fund second property
- **T286**: Target 48 beds total for $500k/year revenue
- **T287**: Acquire 1 new property every 6-12 months
- **T288**: Transition from rental arbitrage to ownership by year 2
- **T289**: Leverage cash flow for down payments
- **T290**: Use income tax refund for new property deposits
- **T291**: House hack initial property (live-in, rent extra rooms)
- **T292**: Calculate ROI: Year 1 rental arbitrage = 274% (Lynette's example)
- **T293**: Aim for $1,600/month profit per 6-bed property
